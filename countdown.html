COUNTDOWN GAME



WHAT IS COUNTDOWN ?

Our game is a game in which our player 'P' tries to score points by smashing numbers on a 23*53 playing field.

WHAT IS THE OBJECTIVE OF THE GAME?

The aim of our game is to get the highest score possible before 'P' runs out of lives.

GAME RULES

  1. P cannot push increasing numbers.
  2. If there is only one number between P and the wall,
    P cannot smash the number.
  3. For each number disappearing from the screen, a new
    number appears, which is initially displayed in yellow.
  4. Numbers decrease by 1 every 15 seconds.
  5. There is a %3 chance that 1 will turn into 0.
  6. 0 moves randomly. If, during one of its moves,
    it lands on the player, the player loses one life.
  7. P starts with 5 lives. The game ends when P runs out
    of lives.
